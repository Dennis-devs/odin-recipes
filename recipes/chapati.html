Chapati

Description

Easy chapati recipe made without yeat or baking powder. you'll love how soft and pliable this Kenyan chapati is, making it perfect to use as a wrap or to dunk into sauces

ingredients;

  • All purpose flour
  • luke warm water
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 1/2 cup ghee or butter or oil

Steps;

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add water oil, sugar, salt and mix well. Add flour and mix with a wooden spoon till it comes together. turn the dough onto a slightly floured surface. kneed the dough for 10-15 min till a smooth and supple dough is achieved
  2. Divide the dough and form them into balls then cover them with a plastic wrap for 15 min
  3. Slightly flour your working surface and using a rolling pin roll out as thin as you can one ball at a time
  4. brush the rolled dough with oil or ghee or butter and roll them again into a ball
  5. cover the doughand allow to rest for 10 min
  6. roll out the balls again one at a time using a rolling pin
  7. heat a skillet over medium-high heat and place the rolled out dough. brush the upper side with oil and turn and do the same to the other side till it browns. Remove and place in a covered container. enjoy!
