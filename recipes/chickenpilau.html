Chicken Pilau

Description

A delicious and tasty recipe for you. Serve it with some kachumbari and enjoy!


ingredients;

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic
  • Chicken pieces
  • salt
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 2 cups water

Steps;

  1. heat oil
  2. add sliced onions and cook till brown
  3. Add grated ginger, garlic then stir
  4. Add chicken pieces, salt and pilau masala then stir
  5. wash the rice and add it to the chicken
  6. Add some water and stir
  7. cover and cook for 15-18 minutes
  8. Once ready serve it up with some kachumbari, enjoy!
