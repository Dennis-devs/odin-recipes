Black Bean Rice



Description:


Rice cooked with black beans in a tasty tomato sauce. Delicious!


Ingredients;

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 cloves
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • salt
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 2 cups water

Steps;

  1. Heat oil in a cooking pot
  2. Add blended tomatoes, garlic, onion
  3. Add paprika, cumin and mixed herbs
  4. Add salt to taste
  5. let cook
  6. Add rice and black beans
  7. Add 2 cups water and cook for 12-15 min
  8. serve with preferred garnish
